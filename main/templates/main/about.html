About BridgeBuddy 🌍

BridgeBuddy is a web platform designed to help international and Erasmus students adapt more easily to life in Türkiye.

Many students face language barriers, transportation confusion, accommodation issues, healthcare communication problems, and difficulty understanding local systems.

This platform provides:

  • Survival Guides
  • Community Support
  • Essential Turkish Phrases
  • Student Discussions
  • Daily Life Assistance
