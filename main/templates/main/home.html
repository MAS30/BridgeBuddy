Why BridgeBuddy?

International students often struggle with language barriers, transportation systems, healthcare communication, accommodation, and adapting to a completely new environment.

BridgeBuddy provides a centralized platform that helps students navigate daily life in Türkiye through survival guides, community support, and essential Turkish communication assistance.
